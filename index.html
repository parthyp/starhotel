Welcome to Star Hotel - Downtown Williams, Arizona on Historic Route 66

Premier Grand Canyon Lodging with Free Wi-Fi, Coffee Maker & Modern Amenities in Every Room

Ideal location for Grand Canyon National Park visitors, Bearizion travelers, and Route 66 adventurers.

Star Hotel is your perfect base camp for exploring the Grand Canyon and surrounding Arizona attractions. Located in downtown Williams on the iconic Route 66, we offer affordable, quality lodging just 50 miles from Grand Canyon National Park. Enjoy a scenic 5-minute walk to the Grand Canyon Railway Station, or explore nearby attractions including Bearizion and the Williams Municipal Golf Course. Whether you're planning a Grand Canyon adventure or a Route 66 road trip, our family-friendly hotel provides everything you need for a comfortable stay.
